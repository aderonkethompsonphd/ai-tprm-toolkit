## Article 20 — Management Body Obligations

- [ ] Management body has formally approved cybersecurity risk management measures
- [ ] Management body oversees implementation of Article 21 measures
- [ ] Management body members have completed cybersecurity training (Article 20(2))
- [ ] Date of last management body cybersecurity review: ___________
- [ ] Management body minutes documenting approval are retained

---

## Article 21(2) — Ten Minimum Measures

### Measure 1 — Risk Analysis and IS Policies
- [ ] Information security policy documented and approved by management
- [ ] Risk assessment methodology documented
- [ ] Risk assessment conducted within last 12 months
- [ ] Risk treatment plan maintained and reviewed
- [ ] Risk register updated at least annually or on significant change

### Measure 2 — Incident Handling
- [ ] Incident Response Plan (IRP) documented
- [ ] Incident severity classification scheme defined
- [ ] Incident response team designated with named roles
- [ ] Notification procedure for significant incidents established
- [ ] Traficom/national CSIRT registration completed (Finland: NCSC-FI)
- [ ] 24-hour early warning capability confirmed
- [ ] 72-hour incident notification capability confirmed
- [ ] IRP tested within last 12 months (tabletop minimum)

### Measure 3 — Business Continuity
- [ ] Business Continuity Plan (BCP) documented
- [ ] Disaster Recovery Plan covering ICT systems documented
- [ ] RTO and RPO defined for essential services
- [ ] Backup management policy in place
- [ ] Backups tested for successful restoration within last 6 months
- [ ] BCP tested within last 12 months
- [ ] Crisis management procedures documented

### Measure 4 — Supply Chain Security
- [ ] Vendor risk classification model in place (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
- [ ] All vendors classified within last 12 months
- [ ] Vendor security questionnaire programme deployed
- [ ] Critical and High tier vendors assessed within last 12 months
- [ ] Security requirements documented contractually in supplier agreements
- [ ] Ongoing reassessment schedule in place (Critical: 6m, High: 12m)
- [ ] Vendor incident notification procedure in place
- [ ] Fourth-party risk visibility for Critical vendors

### Measure 5 — Secure Acquisition, Development, Maintenance
- [ ] Secure development lifecycle (SDLC) requirements documented
- [ ] Vulnerability management programme in place
- [ ] Patch management policy with defined SLAs
- [ ] Security requirements included in IT/OT procurement process
- [ ] Critical vulnerability patching SLA: _______ hours

### Measure 6 — Effectiveness Assessment
- [ ] Cybersecurity KPIs defined and tracked
- [ ] Internal audit programme covers cybersecurity
- [ ] Management review of cybersecurity effectiveness conducted annually
- [ ] Last management review date: ___________

### Measure 7 — Cyber Hygiene and Training
- [ ] Security awareness training programme in place
- [ ] Training frequency: _________ (Minimum: annually; recommended: quarterly)
- [ ] Role-specific security training for IT, OT, and management staff
- [ ] Phishing simulation conducted within last 12 months
- [ ] Management body cybersecurity training completed (Article 20(2))

### Measure 8 — Cryptography and Encryption
- [ ] Cryptography policy documented
- [ ] Encryption standards defined (AES-256 minimum for sensitive data at rest)
- [ ] TLS 1.3 enforced for data in transit
- [ ] Certificate lifecycle management in place
- [ ] CBOM (Cryptography Bill of Materials) completed or scheduled

### Measure 9 — HR Security, Access Control, Asset Management
- [ ] HR security policy (joiners/movers/leavers process) documented
- [ ] Asset inventory maintained and reviewed
- [ ] Privileged access management (PAM) in place
- [ ] Access reviews conducted at least annually
- [ ] Background screening policy in place

### Measure 10 — Multi-Factor Authentication
- [ ] MFA enforced for all remote access (VPN, remote desktop)
- [ ] MFA enforced for all privileged accounts
- [ ] MFA enforced for all cloud service accounts
- [ ] OT remote access: individual credentials + MFA (no shared credentials)
- [ ] Secure communication channels for sensitive information

## Article 21(3) — Supply Chain Security Summary
- [ ] Vendor criticality classification in place
- [ ] Security assessments conducted for critical and high-tier vendors
- [ ] Security requirements in supplier contracts
- [ ] Ongoing vendor monitoring in place
- [ ] Supply chain incident response process defined

## Article 23 — Incident Reporting Readiness
- [ ] Significant incident definition understood and documented
- [ ] 24-hour early warning capability tested
- [ ] 72-hour notification procedure documented and tested
- [ ] Traficom/NCSC-FI reporting portal access confirmed
- [ ] Communication templates prepared for incident notifications
