# DORA ICT Third-Party Risk Checklist
**AI-TPRM Toolkit | Regulation (EU) 2022/2554 Articles 28-44 | Version 1.0 | CC BY 4.0 | Aderonke Thompson**

---

## Article 28 — General Principles

- [ ] ICT TPRM policy documented and approved by management body?
- [ ] Policy includes: risk-based approach, due diligence, contractual requirements
- [ ] Register of all ICT third-party arrangements maintained?
- [ ] Register updated at least annually?
- [ ] Critical ICT third-party service providers identified?

---

## Article 30 — Contractual Requirements

All contracts with ICT third-party providers must include:
- [ ] Clear and complete service description
- [ ] Locations where services are provided and data processed
- [ ] Data security provisions including encryption
- [ ] Availability, integrity, and confidentiality assurances
- [ ] Incident management and reporting (initial: 4 hours, follow-up: 72 hours)
- [ ] Participation in DORA digital operational resilience testing
- [ ] Exit strategy and transition assistance
- [ ] Audit rights (by financial entity and competent authority)
- [ ] Sub-contracting disclosure and flow-down requirements
- [ ] Service level agreements with measurable targets
- [ ] Data portability at contract end

---

## Article 31 — Critical ICT Third-Party Providers

- [ ] Has the vendor been designated as a CTPP by competent authorities?
- [ ] If CTPP: Is the Lead Overseer notified?
- [ ] Sub-contracted services by CTPP disclosed?
- [ ] Risk concentrations from use of this CTPP assessed?

---

## Article 32 — Preliminary Assessment

Before signing contract:
- [ ] Due diligence on security practices completed?
- [ ] Compatibility with ICT security requirements assessed?
- [ ] Concentration risk assessed (multiple critical functions with one provider)?
- [ ] Sub-contracting chain reviewed?

---

## Article 33 — Monitoring ICT Third-Party Risk

Ongoing monitoring requirements:
- [ ] Service performance monitored against SLAs?
- [ ] Security updates and patch management monitored?
- [ ] Audit reports reviewed (SOC 2, ISO 27001, penetration test)?
- [ ] Incident notifications tracked?
- [ ] Periodic reassessment schedule in place?

---

## Article 35 — Exit Strategy

- [ ] Exit strategy documented for this ICT third-party arrangement?
- [ ] Alternative providers identified?
- [ ] Data migration and portability confirmed?
- [ ] Transition period defined?
- [ ] Exit tested or reviewed?
