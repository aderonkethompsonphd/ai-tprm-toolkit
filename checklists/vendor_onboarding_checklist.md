# Vendor Onboarding Checklist
**AI-TPRM Toolkit | ISO 27001 Annex A 5.19-5.22 Aligned | Version 1.0 | CC BY 4.0 | Aderonke Thompson**

---

## Pre-Onboarding (Before Engagement Begins)

- [ ] Vendor need confirmed by requesting team
- [ ] Vendor business details collected (legal name, registration number, country)
- [ ] Sanctions screening completed (WorldCheck or equivalent)
- [ ] Beneficial ownership verified (trade register or equivalent)
- [ ] Public reputation check conducted (news search, adverse media)
- [ ] Risk tier assigned: Critical / High / Medium / Low
- [ ] Risk tier assignment documented with rationale

**Outcome:** CLEAR to proceed | FLAGGED — escalate to Compliance

---

## Questionnaire and Assessment

- [ ] Appropriate questionnaire selected (based on risk tier and vendor type)
- [ ] Questionnaire sent with SLA date
- [ ] Questionnaire receipt acknowledged by vendor
- [ ] SLA chasing reminder set (3 days before overdue)
- [ ] Questionnaire responses received
- [ ] Responses reviewed by Compliance
- [ ] Risk score calculated (see: scoring/vendor_risk_scoring_model.md)
- [ ] Red findings documented and remediation plan requested
- [ ] Risk score below escalation threshold? Yes: continue | No: escalate to senior reviewer

**Risk score:** ___ / 10 | **Escalation required:** Yes / No

---

## Legal and Contractual

- [ ] Does vendor process personal data? Yes / No
  - [ ] If Yes: DPA required and sent to Legal
  - [ ] DPA reviewed against GDPR Article 28 requirements (see: governance/dpa_review_checklist.md)
  - [ ] DPA signed and uploaded to vendor record
- [ ] Security requirements included in supplier agreement?
- [ ] Sub-processor notification clause included?
- [ ] Audit rights clause included?
- [ ] Incident notification requirement included (24-hour SLA)?
- [ ] Data deletion/return at contract end confirmed?
- [ ] Exit strategy / offboarding provisions included?

---

## Sustainability (Energy transition supply chain — if applicable)

- [ ] Sustainability questionnaire section completed?
- [ ] Sustainability credentials reviewed by Sustainability team?
- [ ] Outcome: APPROVED / CONDITIONAL / REJECTED

---

## Final Approval

- [ ] All Red findings resolved or have accepted remediation plan with deadline?
- [ ] DPA signed (if required)?
- [ ] Supplier agreement signed?
- [ ] Sustainability approval confirmed (if applicable)?
- [ ] Final approval granted by: ___________________ Date: ___________
- [ ] Approval recorded with timestamp in vendor management system
- [ ] Vendor status set to: ACTIVE

---

## Post-Approval

- [ ] IT notified to provision access (specify access type and scope)
- [ ] Access type: _________________________________
- [ ] Access provisioned by: ______________ Date: __________
- [ ] Vendor record created/updated in TPRM system
- [ ] Reassessment date set:
  - Critical: 6 months from approval
  - High: 12 months from approval
  - Medium: 24 months from approval
  - Low: 36 months from approval
- [ ] Vendor owner assigned (internal business contact)
