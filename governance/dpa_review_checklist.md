## Pre-Review

- [ ] Vendor confirmed as data processor (processing personal data on our behalf)
- [ ] Type of personal data to be processed:
- [ ] Special categories involved? (Health, genetic, biometric, racial, political, religious, union, sex life)
- [ ] Vendor DPA received or St1 standard DPA to be issued?

---

## Additional Requirements

- [ ] Sub-processor list appended (name, location, purpose, DPA in place)
- [ ] Prior approval required for new sub-processors (not just notification)
- [ ] Security measures specified (not just referenced) — minimum: encryption at rest/transit, access controls, incident notification
- [ ] Data deletion timeline specified (recommend: 30 days maximum, not 90)
- [ ] Audit right is active (not just information provision) — includes right to commission audit
- [ ] Incident notification SLA specified — minimum: without undue delay; recommend: <24 hours
- [ ] Data return option specified (not just deletion)
- [ ] Transfer mechanism specified for non-EEA sub-processors (SCCs, adequacy decision)
- [ ] Governing law and jurisdiction specified
